vocab-ocr











Modes:

default:

Only performs OCR. No pre processing. Use when other modes don't match or give bad results.

pronunciation:

Pre processing:

None.

Post processing:

Removes every line from the output that starts with '[', '|' or some other character that tesseract detects faulty to remove the pronunciation hints.

greenwich:

Pre processing:

Filters out every color but black from the left column and everything but blue from the right column. Tries to remove the horizontal lines.

Post processing:

Removes every line from the output that starts with '[', '|' or some other character that tesseract detects faulty to remove the pronunciation hints.
